Introduction

QUILTBAG: An LGBTQ+ Community Center

QUILTBAG is a community center that provides resources for the LGBTQ+ community. It hosts events, maintains a lounge space, and has resources like books, Pride merchandise, safe sex supplies, and more. These resources are available for purchase, but many can also be accessed via a free lending program or paid for with volunteer work.

Target audience

QUILTBAG's target audience is the LGBTQ+ community; it is particularly targeted at LGBTQ+ youth and those in need.

QUILTBAG's audience uses this site to find resources, connect with community, and learn about events.
