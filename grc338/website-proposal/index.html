Introduction

QUILTBAG: An LGBTQ+ Community Center

QUILTBAG is a community center that provides resources for the LGBTQ+ community. It hosts events, maintains a lounge space, and has resources like books, Pride merchandise, safe sex supplies, and more. These resources are available for purchase, but many can also be accessed via a free lending program or paid for with volunteer work.

Target audience

QUILTBAG's target audience is the LGBTQ+ community; it is particularly targeted at LGBTQ+ youth and those in need.

QUILTBAG's audience uses this site to find resources, connect with community, and learn about events.

Resources

Library: We offer a library of books, movies, audiobooks, magazines, and news articles related to LGBTQ+ topics. These range from educational to entertaining. Our volunteers can help you find just the resource you need.

[A table with books on it.]

Lounge: The QUILTBAG lounge is a space that is open every day of the week from 8am–10pm. You can meet new friends, participate in events, or just have a quiet place to work here.

[A couch with a rainbow flag behind it.]

Events: We host events regularly all year round, and our space is the perfect place for your LGBTQ+ event, too! To reserve our space for your event, speak to a staff member or email us.

Shop: We offer Pride merchandise, menstrual hygiene, safe sex supplies, and local artists' work at affordable prices. We also accept volunteer hours as payment.

Events

LGBTQ+ Youth Group: This group meets every Monday afternoon from 4–5pm. Open to youth ages 12–19.

Trans Group: This group meets every other Friday evening from 8–9pm. Open to transgender, nonbinary, genderqueer, gender nonconforming, and gender questioning individuals.

M-Spec Group: This group meets every Saturday from 10–11am. Open to all; discussion is centered around those who experience attraction in any form to multiple genders of people.

Dungeons and Dragons Group: This group meets every Sunday afternoon from 2–4pm. Open to all.

To schedule your own event in our space, speak with any of our staff!
